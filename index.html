About

I’m Jason Silvia, aka Indigo Monday. I’ve spent more than a decade creating custom tracks and providing production services for everyone from musicians, to entrepreneurs, to businesses, and all around creatives. I host a podcast for creative entrepreneurs called The Creative Capital Show , as well as a bi-weekly music + coffee mixshow on twitch called Double Shot Sunday. You could say I’m a mix of creative, nerdy, and detail-obsessed. I have an often hard-to-satisfy eye and ear, for quality. It’s for these reasons that I’m skilled at what I do. Searching for a sound that’s a bit different? Then you’re in the right place.
